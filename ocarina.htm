SONGS

Song of Time

  • The Song Of Time is used to go back in time to the begining of the first day. You will lose all your progress, except from the masks, items and hearts you already have. It is usually used in the last seconds of the last day to exploit the whole 3 days you have. It can also be used when you have completed all the sidequests you were trying to accomplish, or to try again if you fail because the events are timed.
  • You learn this song as soon as you take back yout ocarina from Skull Kid, remembering the moment Zelda showed it to you.

Song of Double Time

  • The Song of Double Time, is the 3 notes of the Song of Time played twice in a row. It is used to jump foreword in time half a day (to 6.00 am or 6.00 pm)

Inverted Song of Time

  • This song is th Song of Time played backwards, its effect is that the timeflow slows so you can have more time to explore, complete quests or dungueons.

Goron Lullaby

  • The Goron Lullaby has the power to sleep the goron NPCs
  • You have to complete some task to learn this sons, as it is handed to you in two parts:
    • First you need to talk to the crying goron, while wearing the goron mask.
    • The first half is showed to you by the Goron Elder, you have to find in in the snow area, and tell him about the cryign goron.
    • When you know the first half, you have to return to th crying goron and play it before him, so he can teach you the rest of the song
  • You will need this song to enter to the mountain dungeon.

Song of Storms

  • Used to blow away curses or water Magic Beans and make them grow without using a bottle filled with water
  • To learn the song you have to go to the Ikana Graveyard during the first night and talk to one of the Stalchildren, wearing the Captain's Hat, and open the grave.
  • Then you will have to complete the next challenges:
    • Kill all the bats.
    • Light the torches.
    • In the last room, defeat the Iron Knucle

Epona's Song

  • This song makes Epona, your horse, come to you. With Epona you can move quickly and jump over fences.
  • Also if you play it in fron of a cow, you can get milk for free.
  • To lear the song you have to practise with Romani on the first day.

Song of Soaring

  • When you play this song you can fly to the entrance of the dungeons or to the Owl statues you have already awakened
  • The big Owl, Kaepora Gaebora, will teach you this song right before you enter the Woods.

Sonata of Awakening

  • This song have the power to make old or sleeping things wake up.
  • You need to play it to make the first dungeon appear
  • The monkey held prisioner in the Deku Palace teaches you the song

Elegy of Emptiness

  • Used to create a statue of you in the form you played it. Using the three masks you can create four statues.
  • The king of Ikana teaches it to you after defeating him in his castle.

Oath to Order

  • Used to call the Giants to the clock tower when you have freed the four of them.
  • Afer defeating the first boss, Odolwa, and completing the fist dungueon, the first Giant teaches you the song.

Song of Healing

  • The Song of Healing it is used to obtain the main three masks, the Deku, Goron and Zora ones.
  • It has the effect of curing the first curse and giving peace to the souls of the remainig, like the Goron Hero and the Zora musician
  • You can lear this song has soon has you recover your ocarina and went back in time for the first time, when you return to the clock tower the Mask Seller is the one who teaches you the song.

New Wave Bossa Nova

  • Used to awake the Island Turtle.
  • After collecting the seven Zora Eggs, you have to find them in the Gerudo Hideout, they are placed in the tank in the Marine Research Lab. After hatching the Zora Tadpolls, will teach you the song.

Scarecrow Song

  • This is an extra song that will make the Scarecrow appear in some places when played
  • You make this song playing your ocarina in fron of the Scarecrow, it has to be 8 notes long and original, but when you play the Song Of Time, the song will be forgotten and if you want you can make it all over again with the Scarecrow.
